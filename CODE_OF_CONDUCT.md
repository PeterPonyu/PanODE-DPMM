# Code of Conduct

This repository aims to be a respectful, constructive place for research and engineering collaboration.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and assume good intent,
- give technical feedback without personal attacks,
- welcome questions from contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels,
- focus discussions on evidence, reproducibility, and the work itself, and
- respect project boundaries around privacy, security, and unpublished research material.

## Unacceptable behavior

The following are not acceptable in this project space:

- harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or hate speech,
- sexualized language or unwelcome personal attention,
- doxxing, threats, or sustained disruption,
- bad-faith review behavior, including deliberately hostile or mocking feedback, and
- sharing private data, credentials, or sensitive unpublished material without permission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, review threads, and other project-managed communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior, report it privately to the repository maintainer rather than escalating publicly. Include links, screenshots, or minimal reproduction details when possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or moderate content that violates these expectations. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from participation.
